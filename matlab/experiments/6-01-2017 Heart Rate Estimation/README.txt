chest_samples:
Baseband data (186 length vector) collected at 60 frames per second with the radar directed at a male chest 1800 times.)
Two samples were taken (two 186x1800 matricies)

cubic_spline_3d.m:
MATLAB function for performing cubic spline on 3D data (cubic spline fitting of a surface rather than a line)

EstimateCPMotion.mlx:
Live script which analyzes the data (186xn matrix where n is the number of frames taken) to extract respiratory rates and cardiac rates.
